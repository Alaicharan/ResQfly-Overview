Proposed Solution

The First Intelligent Airlift Platform

ResQFly delivers critical medical supplies and communication devices to inaccessible disaster zones, bridging the crucial gap between disaster onset and ground team arrival.

  • AI-Powered Navigation with obstacle avoidance
  • 5kg payload capacity for essential supplies
  • Opportunistic survivor detection during missions
  • Real-time coordination dashboard
  • Works in communication-degraded environments

Key Differentiators

  • 20% faster response time in inaccessible regions
  • No ground infrastructure required
  • 25+ minutes flight time with full payload
  • All-weather, day-night capability
  • 90% cheaper than helicopter operations
  • Ready to fly in under 5 minutes

Technical Approach

Hardware Stack

Tarot T960 Hexacopter, NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano, Bharat FC700 Flight Controller, Dual communication links

AI & Software Stack

YOLOv8 + TensorRT, Visual SLAM, Artificial Potential Fields, Custom CNN for landing

Communication

4G/LTE + 900MHz Telemetry redundancy, MQTT protocol, Cloud-based dashboard

Technical Workflow

Request

Medic requests supplies via mobile app

Dispatch

Cloud server assigns optimal drone

Navigate

AI avoids obstacles, finds optimal path

Detect

Opportunistic human detection during transit

Deliver

Precision payload release at destination

  • Dual communication links for redundancy

  • Modular design with swappable components
  • Low operational costs
